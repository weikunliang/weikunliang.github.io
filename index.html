Hi, I'm Weikun.

I am a designer and developer studying Information Systems and Human Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon. I enjoy creating beautiful designs for the products I build, like applications, websites, posters, etc. I believe that a beautiful design not only facilitates the whole user experience, but also makes it more enjoyable.

In my free time, I’m usually out exploring the city I currently live in and finding new things to try out. I’m a huge fan of food and fashion and I love travelling to new places and experiencing new cities through food and fashion.
